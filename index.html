How to make an awesome Short Film!

Assembly time: Couple of days to a couple of months
Awesome rating: 100% awesome

ALways wanted to start a short film but don't know where to begine? Follow these 7 steps and you're on your way to Hollywood!

  • Things You'll Need

  • A budget
  • A script
  • Camera Equipment
  • Props
  • Location(s) to shoot your film
  • Friends!

  1. Read the script.

    By doing this, you allow the imagery of how the setting, the mood, or the character's emotion come into play.

  2. Break down each scene

    During this part of the process, write or sketch down each scene of how you would like to shoot it. This can also help with the process of organizing which scene you would like to imput from various angles.

    Sketch out your scene!

  3. Go Scout!

    After all of the planning of how you would like to shoot each scene, go start looking for places that satisfies your setting preferences. If you decide to shoot in a public setting, you would often need a permit and it could be difficult with background noises.

  4. Props!

    In this step, start making a list of props you will need for your short film!

  5. Equipment

    As for equipment, work with what have at the moment. Ideally 2-3 cameras and 1 good microphone can help you come a long way as a beginner. Also to take into consideration when filming is that make sure lighting is great, have back up batteries just in case your camera dies, and have extra memory cards for extra storage.

  6. Recruitment

    Once you have most of your layout done, have your equipment ready, now you can start hiring recruiters to help you in this process. Examples: The camera crew, editors, production assistants etc...

  7. Actors

    Now it's time to start casting for actors. Try to find local actors or start posting out flyers about auditions for people to try out for your film!
